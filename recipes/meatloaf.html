Meatloaf Recipe

Recipe Description

This traditional meatloaf recipe made with sauteed vegetables, spices, and bread crumbs is moist and flavorful and topped with an old-fashioned glaze.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium carrot, coarsely chopped
  • 1 rib celery, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 medium onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 medium red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
  • 4 white mushrooms, coarsely chopped
  • 3 gloves garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 2 1/2 pounds ground chuck
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup plain bread crumbs
